Membership: Membership in Aaroh is open to all students who are interested in music, regardless of their major or skill level. Auditions may be required for those who want to perform as part of the club, or it may be more relaxed and open to anyone who wants to participate.

Meetings and events: Aaroh has regular meetings to plan events, discuss music, and share ideas. These events could include concerts, open mic nights, jam sessions, and workshops, where students can showcase their talents and learn from each other.

Performance opportunities: One of the key benefits of being part of Aaroh is the opportunity to perform in front of live audiences. Whether it's a solo performance, a small group, or a full ensemble, students will have the chance to showcase their skills and gain valuable experience performing in front of a crowd.

Collaboration with other organizations: Aaroh may collaborate with other organizations on campus, such as theater groups or dance clubs, to create joint performances and events.
