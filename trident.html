Trident Labs is a college club based in Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), located in Pune, India. The club is dedicated to exploring and developing cutting-edge technology in the fields of AUVs (autonomous underwater vehicles), satellite making, and other sectors related to space and underwater exploration.

Trident Labs is comprised of technical and corporate teams, both of which work together to design and build machines that can be used in various applications. Their main focus is on research and development, with a strong emphasis on important concepts like aerodynamics, hydrodynamics, and control systems.

Trident Labs is divided into two teams, namely the technical and corporate teams, that work together to produce innovative technology. The technical team is responsible for the development of machines and equipment, while the corporate team manages funding, marketing, and promotion. This structure enables the club to develop technology that is both innovative and commercially viable.

In addition to their technical work, Trident Labs is also involved in conducting workshops on various topics. They have organized workshops on the use of Arduino and electronic components, as well as machine learning. These workshops are open to students and professionals interested in learning about the club's technology and research.

Trident Labs has participated in a number of state and national level competitions and has brought laurels to their college.The members of Trident Labs are passionate about exploring the latest technology and its applications in space and underwater exploration. Their work serves as an inspiration to many students and professionals interested in the field of robotics and machine learning. Overall, the club's commitment to research and development and its contribution to the field of technology is impressive and noteworthy.
