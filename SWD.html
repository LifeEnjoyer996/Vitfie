The Social Welfare & Development Committee is a VIT's (Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune) club for social services, which believes in the idea of social service and encourages its members to work towards the same. This club has an animal rescue team and a street play team. The flagship event of this club is called AATMABODH, which is famous for spreading awareness about social issues through street plays.

The club also works towards providing assistance to underprivileged children by providing them with food and promoting literacy.

In addition to these activities, the Social Welfare & Development Committee also helps nearby police forces in providing security during major festivals in Pune, such as Ganpati Visarjan.

They are nature-friendly and also emphasize planting plants. The club's major events/activities include animal rescue, Utkarsh (an event to help underprivileged children), Udaan (a platform for women's empowerment), night patrolling, Avishkar (an event to promote innovation), and Swachha Pune (an initiative to promote cleanliness in the city).

The Social Welfare & Development Committee at VIT is really dedicated to making a positive impact in their community. Blood donation camps and voting awareness campaigns are really important initiatives that can help save lives and promote civic engagement. Webinars can also be a great way to educate and inspire people about important issues. It's great to see a club that is involved in such a wide range of activities and is making a real difference in the world.
