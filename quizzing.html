QSOC (VIT Pune Quizzing Society) is a college club that focuses on promoting the art of quizzing among students. The club provides opportunities for students to participate in quiz competitions, organize and conduct quizzes, and interact with experts and peers in the field.

Membership in QSOC is open to all students interested in quizzing and related activities. The club is managed by a committee of elected members who serve for one year. The committee is responsible for organizing events, managing the club's budget, and promoting the club's activities to the wider student community.

The club's activities are centered around three main pillars: learning, practicing, and competing. Under the learning pillar, QSOC organizes workshops, seminars, and study groups to help students enhance their knowledge and skills in quizzing. The club also organizes lectures and talks by experts in the field to expose students to new topics and trends in quizzing.

Practicing is an important aspect of QSOC's activities, and the club provides opportunities for students to practice and improve their skills through mock quizzes and other activities. The club also organizes intra-club quizzes and competitions to provide a platform for students to test their knowledge and skills.

Competing is another important aspect of QSOC's activities, and the club participates in inter-college quizzes and competitions to showcase the skills and talents of its members. The club has a strong track record of success in national and international competitions, and its members have gone on to become successful quizzers and quizmasters.

In addition to its quizzing activities, QSOC also organizes social events such as movie nights, picnics, and sports tournaments to promote camaraderie and foster friendships among members.

In conclusion, QSOC is a college club that provides students with opportunities to explore and enhance their knowledge and skills in quizzing. Through a range of activities focused on learning, practicing, and competing, QSOC aims to prepare students for successful careers in the field of quizzing and to foster a sense of camaraderie and community among its members.
