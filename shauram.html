Vishwa Shauryam is the official defense club of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), located in Pune, India. This club focuses on promoting and creating awareness about defense-related matters, such as military life, defense exams, and other aspects of the defense industry.

Vishwa Shauryam places great emphasis on discipline and instilling a sense of responsibility and duty among its members. The club conducts training programs and workshops that focus on improving the physical and mental fitness of its members, and encourages them to adopt a disciplined and structured lifestyle.

The club also provides guidance and support to students who aspire to pursue a career in the defense sector. Vishwa Shauryam are willing to conduct coaching classes for defense exams, and provides information about various job opportunities in the defense industry. This helps aspiring defense cadets to get a head start on their career path.

One of the major events organized by the club is Garjana, which is a talk show where defense personnel are invited to speak about their military life, the hardships they have faced, and the victories they have witnessed. This event provides an excellent opportunity for students to learn about the real-life experiences of defense personnel and gain insights into the challenges of serving in the defense industry.

The members of Vishwa Shauryam are highly motivated and dedicated individuals who are committed to serving their country. The club's focus on instilling discipline, promoting awareness about defense-related matters, and supporting aspiring defense cadets has helped many students achieve their career goals and make a meaningful contribution to their country.

Overall, Vishwa Shauryam has played a significant role in promoting the importance of defense and instilling a sense of duty and responsibility among students at VIT. The club's focus on discipline, training, and awareness has been a source of inspiration for many students who aspire to serve their country and pursue a career in the defense industry.
