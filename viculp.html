Viculp is a cultural production group at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) in Pune, India, dedicated to promoting theatre, drama, and music among students. The club provides a platform for students to showcase their artistic talents and engage in creative activities.

The club participates in various theatre, drama, and music events within and outside the college, including inter-college competitions and festivals. Viculp is well-known for organizing plays and drama events within the college, which not only entertain but also convey meaningful messages to the audience.

Viculp members work collaboratively to produce plays that have a social message or commentary on contemporary issues. These plays are written and directed by club members and involve active participation from students in various roles, such as actors, stage designers, lighting and sound operators, and costume designers.

Apart from plays, Viculp also conducts workshops and training sessions to enhance the skills of its members in various areas of theatre, such as acting, writing, directing, and stagecraft. These sessions are conducted by experienced theatre professionals and experts in the field, providing valuable opportunities for students to learn and grow as artists.

In addition to theatre, Viculp also participates in music events, including inter-college competitions, and festivals. The club has a talented group of musicians who perform at various events and shows, entertaining and inspiring the audience with their music.

Overall, Viculp is an important part of the cultural life at VIT Pune, providing a space for students to explore their artistic interests, develop their skills, and engage with meaningful messages through theatre, drama, and music.
