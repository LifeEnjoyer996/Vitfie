Production & Industrial Engineering Student Association (PIESA) is a college club that is focused on providing students with opportunities to develop their skills in production and industrial engineering. The club aims to provide a platform for students to interact with professionals in the field, gain practical experience through projects and events, and enhance their academic knowledge through seminars and workshops.

Membership in PIESA is open to all students pursuing a degree in production and industrial engineering or related fields. The club's activities are overseen by a committee made up of elected members who serve for one year. The committee is responsible for organizing events, managing the club's budget, and promoting the club's activities to the wider student community.

The club's activities are centered around four main pillars: academics, industry, community service, and social events. Under the academics pillar, PIESA organizes seminars, workshops, and study groups to help students enhance their academic knowledge and prepare for exams. Industry-focused activities include company visits, guest lectures, and projects that allow students to work on real-world problems and gain practical experience in the field.

Community service is an important aspect of PIESA's activities, and the club organizes events such as blood donation drives, health and safety campaigns, and environmental cleanups to give back to the community. Social events are also an important part of PIESA's activities, and the club organizes events such as movie nights, picnics, and sports tournaments to promote camaraderie and foster friendships among members.

PIESA also participates in inter-college events and competitions to showcase the skills and talents of its members. The club has a strong track record of success in national and international competitions, and its members have gone on to successful careers in production and industrial engineering.

In conclusion, Production & Industrial Engineering Student Association (PIESA) is a college club that provides students with opportunities to develop their skills in production and industrial engineering. Through a range of activities focused on academics, industry, community service, and social events, PIESA aims to prepare students for successful careers in the field and to foster a sense of camaraderie and community among its members.
