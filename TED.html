TEDxVITPune is the official and independent organized TED event at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), located in Pune, India. The club is an independent and self-organized group that is licensed by TED to conduct TED-style events in the community.

The club organizes a talk show where various guests are invited to speak about a wide range of topics, such as their life journeys, hardships, skills they have learned, how they achieved their dreams, and many other inspiring topics. These speakers are typically experts in their respective fields, and they share their experiences and knowledge with the audience in a unique and engaging way.

The club's primary focus is to create a platform for sharing ideas and to inspire positive change in the community. TEDxVITPune is committed to organizing events that are thought-provoking, engaging, and informative. The club invites speakers who have a passion for making a difference in the world, and who have unique and inspiring stories to share with the audience.

The talks organized by TEDxVITPune are typically short and concise, lasting around 18 minutes each. The talks cover a wide range of topics, including technology, science, social issues, politics, art, and much more. The aim of the talks is to provide the audience with a fresh perspective on various issues and to inspire them to take action and make a positive difference in their communities.

TEDxVITPune has become a popular club among the students at VIT, and it has helped to foster a culture of innovation, creativity, and social responsibility. The club has inspired many students to pursue their dreams and to make a difference in the world. The events organized by TEDxVITPune provide a unique platform for students to connect with experts in their fields and to learn from their experiences and insights.

Overall, TEDxVITPune has become an integral part of the VIT community, and it has played a significant role in promoting innovative ideas, positive change, and social responsibility among students. The club has successfully created a platform for sharing ideas and inspiring positive change in the community, and it continues to be a source of inspiration and motivation for students at VIT.
