Vclick is the official photography club of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) in Pune, India. The club is dedicated to promoting photography among students and provides a platform for them to showcase their artistic talents.

The club has a team of talented photographers who organize various events, workshops, and photowalks to enhance the skills of its members and encourage their passion for photography. The workshops cover a range of topics, such as camera handling, lighting, composition, and post-processing techniques. They are conducted by experienced photographers and professionals in the field, providing valuable learning opportunities for the members.

Apart from workshops, Vclick also conducts photowalks in and around Pune. These photowalks are organized to explore the city's landmarks, historical sites, and scenic locations, allowing members to capture some of the most beautiful moments of their surroundings.

The club is open to all students who are passionate about photography and is always looking for new members to join. Vclick provides a platform for budding photographers to showcase their work and receive constructive feedback from experienced photographers.

In addition to organizing events and workshops, Vclick also participates in various inter-college and intra-college photography competitions and exhibitions. The club has won several awards and accolades for its outstanding photography work.

Overall, Vclick is an essential part of the cultural and artistic life at VIT Pune, providing a space for students to explore their passion for photography, develop their skills, and engage with like-minded individuals.
