TEAM Griffin is a college club dedicated to the design and construction of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). The club comprises a team of experienced students who are assigned to various domains and work together to bring their models to reality. The club's goal is to design and build UAVs that are efficient, effective, and affordable, while also contributing to the advancement of technology.

The club's experienced team has a diverse set of skills and knowledge, including expertise in aerospace engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science. This multidisciplinary team works cohesively to design and build UAVs that are capable of performing a wide range of tasks, from surveillance and reconnaissance to cargo delivery and search and rescue operations.

All analysis is done in-house to ensure that the design is validated and meets the necessary specifications. The team uses various software tools to simulate and analyze the performance of their models, such as CAD software for 3D modeling and simulation, as well as specialized software for aerodynamic analysis and flight simulation. This analysis helps the team to optimize their designs for maximum efficiency and performance.

Once the design has been validated, the team hands it over to the manufacturing and avionics experts to produce the aircraft. The manufacturing team is responsible for sourcing materials, building the airframe, and installing the necessary components, such as the propulsion system, control surfaces, and avionics. The avionics team is responsible for designing and integrating the onboard electronics, such as the autopilot, sensors, and communication systems.

The club is actively involved in various competitions and events related to UAV design and construction. These competitions provide an excellent opportunity for the club members to showcase their skills, network with industry professionals, and learn from other teams. The club has won several awards and accolades for its innovative designs and outstanding performance in these competitions.

Overall, TEAM Griffin is an excellent opportunity for students interested in aerospace engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science to gain hands-on experience in UAV design and construction. The club's multidisciplinary team and in-house analysis capabilities provide students with a unique opportunity to work collaboratively and bring their designs to reality. The club's participation in competitions and events also provides students with opportunities to showcase their skills, learn from others, and contribute to the advancement of technology.
