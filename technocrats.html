Technocrats is a club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), located in Pune, India. This club is dedicated to gaming, web development, Android, and coding. The club offers a platform for students to explore various fields of computer science, such as app development, web development, game design, and coding. Technocrats focuses on conducting workshops and learning sessions on various coding languages and technologies.

The club provides an opportunity for students to learn about web development, Android app development, game design, and other related fields. Students explore various technologies, such as ethical hacking, blockchain development, app development, and more. Through these workshops, students learn about coding languages like Java, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Technocrats has conducted game design workshops where students learn about designing and programming games using tools like Unity and Unreal Engine. The club also conducts website design workshops where students learn about designing and developing websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

The club provides a platform for students to showcase their skills and compete with others. They organize coding competitions where students can demonstrate their coding skills and compete with others. Technocrats also participates in various coding competitions at the state and national level and has won laurels for their college.

Overall, Technocrats is a vibrant community of students who are passionate about computer science and technology. The club is a great platform for students to learn about app development, web development, game design, and other related fields. The club is dedicated to providing a platform for students to explore and learn new technologies and to develop their skills in various fields of computer science.
