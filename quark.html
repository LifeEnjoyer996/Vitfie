Quark is a college club that focuses on promoting and enhancing the skills of students in the field of science and technology. The club provides opportunities for students to explore the world of science and technology, participate in competitions and events, and interact with experts and peers in the field.

Membership in Quark is open to all students pursuing a degree in science and technology or related fields. The club is managed by a committee of elected members who serve for one year. The committee is responsible for organizing events, managing the club's budget, and promoting the club's activities to the wider student community.

The club's activities are centered around three main pillars: academics, industry, and innovation. Under the academics pillar, Quark organizes workshops, seminars, and study groups to help students enhance their academic knowledge and prepare for exams. The club also organizes lectures and talks by experts in the field to expose students to cutting-edge research and industry trends.

Industry-focused activities include company visits, internships, and projects that allow students to gain practical experience and develop their skills in real-world settings. Quark also partners with industry leaders to organize hackathons and other events that challenge students to use their skills to solve real-world problems.

Innovation is an important aspect of Quark's activities, and the club encourages its members to explore new ideas and develop innovative solutions to complex problems. The club organizes innovation challenges, competitions, and exhibitions to showcase the creative talents of its members.

Quark also participates in inter-college events and competitions to showcase the skills and talents of its members. The club has a strong track record of success in national and international competitions, and its members have gone on to successful careers in science and technology.

In addition to its academic and industry-focused activities, Quark also organizes social events such as movie nights, picnics, and sports tournaments to promote camaraderie and foster friendships among members.

In conclusion, Quark is a college club that provides students with opportunities to explore the world of science and technology, develop their skills, and interact with experts and peers in the field. Through a range of activities focused on academics, industry, and innovation, Quark aims to prepare students for successful careers in the field and to foster a sense of camaraderie and community among its members.
