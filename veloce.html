Veloce Racing India is a prestigious college club that is an official formula student team. The club is dedicated to designing, building, and racing high-performance formula cars, and participating in various competitions around the world. The team also holds the national championship for 2023, adding to their impressive track record.

The club is made up of students from a variety of disciplines, including engineering, design, and business. Members of Veloce Racing India work together in teams to design and build a competitive formula car, using their skills and knowledge to create a high-performance vehicle that can compete with the best in the world.

Once the car is built, the club participates in various formula racing competitions nationally. These competitions are a showcase of innovation, engineering excellence, and driving skill, and Veloce Racing India is known for its competitive edge, pure dedication and hard work.

In addition to racing, Veloce Racing India also focuses on developing the skills of its members, providing them with opportunities to learn about engineering design, project management, and teamwork. The club emphasizes the importance of learning through hands-on experience, and members work closely with industry professionals to gain insights into the world of formula racing.

Overall, Veloce Racing India is a prestigious and highly respected club that is dedicated to excellence in the field of formula racing. With a focus on innovation, teamwork, and skill development, the club provides its members with opportunities to grow both personally and professionally, while representing their college and country on the world stage.
